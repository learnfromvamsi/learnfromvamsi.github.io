Proverbs(సామెతలు),Idioms & Quotes


  • One of the best ways humans figured out to pass their knowledge to the next generations is through Proverbs and Sayings, at least in philosophical perspective.

  • At some point of time we have all seen workers using leveling pipe in construction works.It is based on the Proverb "Water always finds its own level"...People have been using that as a technique for thousands of years...For example,The Romans constructed Aqueducts using that principle 2000 years back.

  • An idiom is an expression or phrase used to convey a particular meaning that would otherwise be a different one when considered the real meanings of the words used in that particular expression or phrase.

  • Ex:- the idiom "piece of cake" means "To be easy"..But when the original meanings of the words are considered the meaning would be different.
